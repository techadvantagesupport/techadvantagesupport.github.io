BudgeTrak SYNC

Budget together, privately

Most shared-budget apps put your family's finances on someone else's dashboard. SYNC shares one budget across up to five devices — encrypted end-to-end, so only your household can read it.

  • Real-time across devicesA purchase logged on one phone updates everyone's available cash in seconds.
  • Encryption our servers can't breakEvery field is encrypted on your phone before upload. The cloud only ever relays ciphertext.
  • Who spent whatPer-member attribution and presence indicators keep the whole household on the same page.
  • One admin, zero chaosOne device administers shared settings; the rest follow automatically. Joining is free for everyone.
